Terrible People

Privacy Information

Note: This privacy policy applies to this instance of Terrible People (The-Circle community). Other servers may have different logging policies.

Temporary Logs

Logs containing your IP address are collected and kept for a few weeks to assist with server monitoring. These logs are not correlated to your username and are not shared with third parties.

Permanent Logs

Gameplay details are kept permanently to analyze card popularity. These logs never contain personally identifiable information, but may include:

  • A temporary ID to correlate cards played during a session
  • A persistent ID to correlate activity between sessions (you can opt out in Preferences)
  • Rough geographic location (city, state, country, postal code)
  • Browser name and language
  • Device operating system and type (phone, tablet, PC, etc.)

Your IP address, chosen username, and chat are never shared with anyone. Fill-in-the-blank card text is logged and shared.
